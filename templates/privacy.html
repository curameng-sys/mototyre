DATA PRIVACY
POLICY.

Effective: January 1, 2025 Last updated: April 2025 RA 10173 Compliant

1 Our Commitment

MotoTyre is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines and its Implementing Rules and Regulations. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

2 Data We Collect

When you register and use MotoTyre, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name, email address, and mobile phone number
  • Motorcycle plate number and motorcycle model (optional at registration)
  • Booking details — service type, date, time, odometer reading, and notes
  • Order history, delivery address, and payment method (no card or GCash credentials stored)
  • Profile picture (optional — uploaded voluntarily by you)
  • Login session tokens and OTP records (temporarily stored, auto-expired)

3 Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal data is collected and processed for the following specific, legitimate purposes:

  • Creating and managing your MotoTyre customer account
  • Processing and confirming service bookings and product orders
  • Sending appointment reminders and order status notifications
  • Sending OTP codes for secure login and email address verification
  • Sending order receipts and payment confirmation emails
  • Assigning mechanics and managing shop operations
  • Improving platform features and overall user experience

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may process your data as part of delivering our platform:

  • Google Gmail API — used to send OTP codes, receipts, and booking notifications from our shop email address
  • PayMongo — used to process GCash payments securely. Your payment credentials are entered directly on PayMongo's platform and are never stored by MotoTyre

These services operate under their own privacy policies. MotoTyre does not store your GCash account details, card numbers, or payment credentials of any kind.

5 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured database. We implement the following security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt cryptographic hashes — never stored in plain text
  • OTP codes automatically expire after 2 minutes and are marked as used immediately after verification
  • Session tokens are secured via Flask-Login and are invalidated upon logout
  • All user inputs are sanitized to prevent SQL injection and cross-site scripting attacks
  • Access to the admin panel is restricted to authorized personnel only

6 Your Rights Under RA 10173

As a data subject under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed — to know how your personal data is being processed
  • Right to Access — to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to Erasure — to request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Right to Object — to object to the processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to File a Complaint — with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at privacy.gov.ph

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at mototyre0505@gmail.com.

7 Cookies & Sessions

MotoTyre uses server-side session cookies (via Flask-Login) solely to keep you authenticated while you use our platform. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or behavioral analytics tools. Session data is cleared when you log out or when the session expires.

8 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active and as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Upon a verified deletion request, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law or for legitimate business records such as completed transaction histories.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the platform or via email. Continued use of MotoTyre after an update constitutes your acknowledgment of the revised policy.
